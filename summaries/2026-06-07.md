### OpenAI unveils Lockdown Mode to protect sensitive data from prompt injection attacks - (TechCrunch)

https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/06/openai-unveils-lockdown-mode-to-protect-sensitive-data-from-prompt-injection-attacks/

# Summary

OpenAI has launched Lockdown Mode, a new security feature designed to protect sensitive data from prompt injection attacks—where malicious instructions are hidden in webpages or other content sources. When enabled, the mode disables live web browsing, image retrieval, deep research, and agent mode, restricting users to cached content only. The feature is currently rolling out to ChatGPT Business accounts and eligible personal accounts.

However, OpenAI acknowledges that Lockdown Mode is not a complete solution, as prompt injections could still appear in cached content or uploaded files. The company emphasizes that the feature is specifically designed for organizations and individuals handling sensitive data who want stricter protection against data exfiltration risks, rather than being intended for general users.

# Summary

On February 28, 2026, McCoy College of Education hosted an in-person training workshop for 48 Dagaare-speaking students to recruit volunteers for expanding Dagaare Wikipedia content. Participants learned about Dagaare Wikipedia, created user accounts, and received instruction on how to create and edit articles. A follow-up online meeting was scheduled but encountered technical difficulties.

Following the workshop, organizers launched a two-week contest challenging the newly trained participants to create 70 articles, resulting in 28 successfully completed articles. This initiative aims to increase and preserve content in the Dagaare language on Wikipedia.
