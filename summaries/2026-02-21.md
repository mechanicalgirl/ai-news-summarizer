## https://0byte.io/articles/pytorch_introduction.html

Python Weekly

# Summary

PyTorch is a popular open-source deep learning framework developed by Meta AI that uses **tensors** as its fundamental data structure for storing and manipulating numbers. Tensors are specialized containers that come with built-in mathematical operations and can be initialized in various ways (torch.rand(), torch.zeros(), etc.). All data types—whether numbers, words, images, or 3D meshes—must be converted to numerical representations that PyTorch can process through tensors.

At the core of neural network training is **autograd**, PyTorch's automatic differentiation engine that calculates gradients (derivatives across multiple variables) without manual computation. These gradients are essential for gradient descent optimization, which iteratively adjusts model parameters to minimize loss. By simply enabling gradient tracking with `requires_grad=True` and calling `.backward()`, PyTorch automatically computes all necessary derivatives, enabling efficient training of neural networks with millions of parameters through repeated cycles of gradient calculation and parameter adjustment.
